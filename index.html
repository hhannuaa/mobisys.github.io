Based on the Safebox technology, we have developed an advanced mobile security solution that allows mobile apps to run in a safer place.

About SAFEBOX

Safebox is a novel app-level virtualization enviroment in which mobile Apps can run safely under your full control without rooting mobile devices or rewriting the apps!

By encapsulating mobile apps in an isolated app-level virtual machine, Safebox provides the capability to enforce more stringent security polices without the necessity to modify the code of apps or the underlying frameworks/kernels, and thereby overcoming the deployment problems that existing security solutions have been facing. Furthermore, mobile apps can be encrypted and run without installation, thus preventing reverse engineering and enabling the capability to bind specific apps to a unique device.

WHY SAFEBOX

You know what? Unlike PC, mobile devices that you own are fully controlled by manufacturers. Not you!

When you install a third-party mobile app, you grant permissions it declared. However, you do not know if those permissions are abused!

What you can do is to trust manufactuers and app developers not to steal your private or business information. In factor, bad vendors and developers largely exist and are using your privacy to make profit now.

Safebox offers a strong weapon to protect your security and privacy. For enterprises, safebox can encrypt your business apps, bind them to specific devices, regulate their behaviors to prevent backdoors, and manage the apps with MDM. For individuals, you can test-run untrusted apps without installing them in the system, and enforce context-aware permission controls.

KEY FEATURES

Without relying on phone manufacturers to update security policies, you can fully control the way mobile apps work in an isolated virtual environment through the safebox app now.

App Encryption

Both app code and data are securely stored in ciphertext and decrypted at run-time in Safebox

Dual-mode Run

Run apps that have been already installed in the system or directly launch apps without installation

Fine Permission Control

Context-aware permission control based on different time, location, and users

Isolation & Seperation

Seperate private and business apps and data into isolated running environments: inside and outside

Cloud Anaylsis

Record the interaction between apps and the system and analyze the potential malicous behavior

Easy Migration

Seamless migration to new devices. All App data are directly available with all data set up.

Remote Management

Remotely install, uninstall, upate, lock, and erase apps or data without user interaction

Compatibility

Install Safebox as a normal app without need to root the device or modify other apps
